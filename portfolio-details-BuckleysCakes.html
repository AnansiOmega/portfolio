Buckleys Cakes

Buckleys Cakes is a website that showcases Clients products, qualifications, and incorporates an email feature that sends orders straight to her business email. I utilized a design system to fully 'furnish' the website. Incorporated multi-image-carousel that was responsive to the users viewport. I also, created a Flickr account for client so she has a user friendly CRUD environemnt. I took this Freelance project as a challenge to create a professional website with all of the expected functionality i.e a dynamic navbar, responsive to users viewport, clean and visually appealing styles throughout.
